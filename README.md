## The Problem

Most people answer "tell me about yourself" by reciting their resume out loud. That's not an introduction — that's a summary nobody asked for.

A great self-introduction tells a story:
- **Who you are** as a professional
- **What you bring** to the table
- **Why you're here** — and why that matters

---

## What This Tool Does

AI guides you through a series of simple questions, then helps you shape your answers into a polished, natural-sounding introduction — one that fits you and the role you're going for.

Works for **any position** — PM, engineer, designer, marketer, or anything in between.
